Version of 'Simon Says: Colorines' optimized for use in neurostimulation treatments.

A large number of variables that professionals can adjust according to their needs:
· Number of buttons: 1–9
· Type of buttons: Numbers, colors, or position only.
· Distractors: Frequency of appearance.
· Speed.
· Order: Normal or reversed.
· Blind mode: the sequence is not repeated each turn.
· Button movement.
